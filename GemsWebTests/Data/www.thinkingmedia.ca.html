What ThinkingMedia Is All About!

ThinkingMedia is a small but growing technical and creative studio. We specialize in bringing together visual elements (video, 3D animation and motion graphics) with the technology to deliver that media interactively using modern technology, such as PHP, C#, Java and Flash. We are unique because we have software developers and creative artists who are able to switch roles during a project to allow for seamless integration of technology and creativity.

Why You Need ThinkingMedia

We make it possible for businesses to quickly raise their technological and creative standards. Simply by out-sourcing their 3D animation projects to our studio they gain years of experience, a carefully assembled team, and confidence that their project will succeed. We have the people, technology, equipment and the production pipeline in place now. By out-sourcing to our business you take advantage of a studio environment ready to start your project.

Services And Skills

  • OOP Programming in C#, Java, Python, PHP and JavaScript.
  • JavaScript development with Dart and TypeScript.
  • Web design styles: CSS, SASS and LESS.
  • Frameworks used: CakePHP, NodeJS, Angular and Backbone.
  • Development tools: Closure, PhpStorm, and Visual Studio with Resharper.
  • Mobile development kits: Android SDK and Xamarin.

3D Animation

ThinkingMedia is a fully equipped 3D animation studio that uses the latest technology and hardware to deliver professional quality 3D animation for use in film, television, gaming and website design. Our creative services include the following.

  • 3D animation sequences and visual effects.
  • Broadcast Design design from concept to delivery.
  • Character animation, 2D animation and story telling.
  • 3D models and modeling service.
